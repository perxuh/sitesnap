SITE SNAP CODEX BUILD INSTRUCTIONS

Project name: SiteSnap
Tagline: Your business website, built from your phone.

I want to build a mobile app called SiteSnap.

SiteSnap is a phone-first AI website builder and website manager for local small business owners, especially contractors and blue-collar service businesses.

The goal is simple:

A business owner downloads the app, answers a few simple questions, uploads photos, chooses a domain, previews the site, and launches a professional website from their phone.

This should not feel like Wix, Webflow, Figma, Lovable, Framer, or WordPress. Those tools still require the user to understand website editing, layouts, publishing, domains, and design choices.

SiteSnap should feel like a guided assistant.

The user should not need technical knowledge.
